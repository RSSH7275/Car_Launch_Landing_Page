The new MG Hector. Undisputed. Ultimate.

There's nothing like having the new MG Hector around. The new design appears crisper, more contemporary, and innovative at first view. Accurate body lines highlight the dominant position. With opulent surfaces emphasizing the sportiness, the high-end SUV has a commanding, elegant, and fascinating appearance. that is Hector's signature.



The new MG Hector - Ultimate features

Traction control modes (snow, mud, sand)

Seamlessly integrated infotainment and cluster screen

All new premium seat with piping

Paddle shifters

Choice of transmission (MT, AT, DCT & IVT)

Choice of engine (1.5l PL, 1.5l DSL, 1.5l Turbo PL)

Based on MG's "Sensuous Sportiness" global design language, the new MG HECTOR has a more assertive attitude and a striking appearance. The new MG HECTOR, with its bold and contemporary exterior and a combination of practical and luxurious interiors, is the ideal SUV to meet consumers' needs for city driving while also satisfying their inner daredevil. The new MG HECTOR has an attractive and robust appearance. Its high hood and redesigned radiator grille give it a commanding front appearance that makes it stand out on the road.

MG Hector is available in Candy White With Starry Black colour. Hector is also available in 7 colours - Havana Grey, Candy White With Starry Black, Starry Black, Aurora Silver, Glaze Red, Dune Brown and Candy White.
